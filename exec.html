Executive Board

President | Anna Peterson

president.gtaoe@gmail.com
As President, Anna is responsible for handling all chapter activities and members, as well as leading the rest of the executive board. Anna is a third year Civil Engineering Major and Global Engineering Leadership Minor from Greensboro, North Carolina. Outside of Alpha Omega Epsilon, she plays Alto Sax in the Georgia Tech Marching Band, Clarinet in the Concert Band and has studied abroad at Georgia Tech Lorraine.

Vice-President | Maddy Shelton

vicepresident.gtaoe@gmail.com
As Vice President, Maddy is in charge of our professional development activities and sisterhood events throughout the year. She is a fourth year Materials Science & Engineering major from Charlotte, NC. She has participated in undergraduate research, co-oped at OFS Fitel, studied abroad, is currently a competitive member of the Georgia Tech Barbell Club powerlifting team, and will be interning with Tesla summer 2018. Maddy enjoys the gym, cooking, and volunteering with animal rescues.

Membership Educator | Dasha Artsikhovska

membership.gtaoe@gmail.com
As membership educator, Dasha is responsible for our recruitment events, guiding new members and welcoming them into the sisterhood. She is a third year Material Science Engineer originally from Ukraine! Outside of A.O.E., she has worked on campus for the past two years on the Nuclear Forensics Research team and she is involved in Georgia Tech’s German Club with which she is planning on Studying Abroad Summer 2018 in Germany and Switzerland.

Treasurer | Kathryn Farley

treasurer.gtaoe@gmail.com
As Treasurer, Kathryn is in charge of the chapter's budget, oversees each officer's budgets, and ensures that all sisters pay dues. Kathryn is a third year Biomedical Engineering major from Ellijay, Ga. Outside of Alpha Omega Epsilon she is the Science and Technology director at Camp Rainey Mountain, a certified BSA Climbing and High Ropes instructor, member of the Women's Recruitment Team at Georgia Tech, and previously served as a fundraising chair for the Society of Women Engineers. Kathryn enjoys camping, teaching, and adventures of all kinds.

Secretary | Meenal Kumar

secretary.gtaoe@gmail.com
As Secretary, Meenal is responsible for taking meeting minutes and distributing them to the sorority. She also helps coordinate logistics as part of the exexcutive board. Meenal is a third year Computer Science major from New Jersey and is involved with the Women in Computing outside of Alpha Omega Epsilon.
