Dress for Success

Alpha Omega Epsilon's main philanthropy is Dress For Success, a nonprofit organization dedicated to promoting the economic independence of disadvantaged women by providing professional attire, a network of support, and career development tools to help women thrive in work and in life. The Sigma Chapter has hosted run for success 5Ks, professional attire fashion shows, and formal tea's in support of Dress for Success. In the past few semesters, the Sigma Chapter has also collects professional attire to donate to Dress for Success.


Members of the Sigma Chapter can often be found giving back in other capacities to Georgia Tech and the greater Atlanta area. AΩE participates in Team Buzz and Tech Beautification Day every year and collaborates with other campus organizations to support their respective causes. Many members have been involved with SWE and their Introduce a Girl to Engineering Day, have volunteered at robotics competitions, and other events that encourage participation in STEM. Other organizations that AΩE members have volunteered with include Atlanta Mission, MedShare, and the Atlanta Humane Society.
