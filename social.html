Sisterhood

The bond of sisterhood is a special one, and we enjoy collaborating and socializing with all of our sisters at Georgia Tech.

Greek Ties

AΩE is proud to be a part of the Georgia Tech Collegiate Panhellenic Council. We serve in an associate role and work with our other associate member, Alpha Delta Chi, and the National Panhellenic Council sororities for the betterment of our campus and the advancement of Greek life.

Community Involvement

Whether it be Homecoming, volunteering, Greek Week, or student organizations - our sisters find that the best way to enhance their experiences at Georgia Tech is to stay as involved in the community as possible!
